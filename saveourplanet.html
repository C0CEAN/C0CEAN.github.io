C0CEAN - Code for the OCEAN

I am a programmer and would like to support environmental protection.

Every year, 400 million tons of plastic are produced worldwide. Up to 18.000 pieces of plastic float on square kilometer of ocean surface.

Each year, up to 135.000 marine animals and one million seabirds die from trash floating in the ocean. The animals starve to death with full stomachs, get caught in old fishing nets, drown or suffer serious injuries during rescue attempts.

Environmental protection is becoming increasingly important. Our future generations are more affected by the climate crisis than we are. The weather is getting hotter, storms are getting more severe, water shortages, famine and much more will still be a major challenge.

We act today for tomorrow. That's why I donate 50% of my earnings to non-profit organizations that work to protect the environment.

You use my software or play my game and additionally support the environment if you buy it.

In addition, I would appreciate a small donation, of which 50% will also be donated. This is absolutely voluntary and any amount is welcome.
